Quantum Ace

Ever Changing Combat

Quantum Ace is a multiplayer starfighter game that merges air combat and card game mechanics. Each starfighter has an large fluctuating array of weapons, maneuvers, and abilities. Each time one is used, it's replaced. Opening a new path to victory.

Pilots must adapt in real time to these options to out fly their opponents and achieve victory.

Fully Loaded Starfighters

While their systems are in constant flux, each fighter is packed to the brim with advanced systems and weaponry. Booming cannons, scores of missiles, searing energy beams, impenetrable shields, confounding stealth systems and dizzying booster engines; each battle will be a complex weave of impossible manuevers, advanced weaponry and cunning tricks.

New Gameplay but Old School Simplicity

Simplified flight controls allow you to focus on battling your enemy, not your controller. Splitscreen and online multiplayer ensure you can find matches near and far.
